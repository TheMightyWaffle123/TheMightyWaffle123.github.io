Purposes

There are many reasons why robots would be useful in our society. One could be to reduce human labor and reduce cost. Another is the impact robots could have on quality. Some aplications robots could be better than is: gluing, spraying(glue or paint), trimming and de-burring, testing and gauging, assembly of appliances, and laboratory routines. If everyone had a car that was droven by a robot, nobody would try to run the red light. Therefore, there would be less car accidents.

Some would argue that a human operator would be better than a robot operator on quality or speed, but a robot operator would not neeed a break and do the task consistently. Also, it would not need to be paid.

Future Purposes

Robots in the future would do the three Ds, dirty, dangerous, or dull, work. They would do work inaccessible to humans, like outer space, deep undersea, mountainous or desert land areas. They could do housework, like ironing, making beds, cleaning the household, gardening, mowing, anything to keep the house tidy. They could even wash your cars.
